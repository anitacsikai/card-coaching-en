Digital Coaching Cards
User Guide

Welcome!

Digital Coaching Cards is an online self-reflection and personal development tool that uses metaphorical image cards to support reflection, insight, and the discovery of new perspectives.

Images can help you explore a life situation, relationship, decision, or inner process in ways that are often difficult to access through words alone.

The platform can be used for individual self-reflection, in pairs, in groups, or as a supportive tool during coaching, counseling, or other guided conversations.

The cards do not provide ready-made answers. Instead, they offer new perspectives, encourage reflection, and support the emergence of personal insights.

What's Included?

Women's Card Deck
90 metaphorical image cards for feminine qualities, emotions, relationships, and inner processes.

Men's Card Deck
90 metaphorical image cards for roles, strengths, relationship patterns, and personal dynamics.

Resource Card Deck
72 metaphorical image cards focused on strengths, opportunities, and personal growth.

Menu Options

1. Visible Selection
All cards are displayed face up. Choose between 1 and 5 cards. Cards can be freely rearranged.

2. Face-Down Draw
All cards are face down. Select 1–5 cards without seeing the images. Cards are revealed after selection.

3. Spreads
12 pre-designed spreads for various self-reflection themes.

4. Custom Spread
Create your own layout — choose rows, card count, and which deck to use per row.

Pre-Designed Spreads

Daily Compass (1) • Self-Reflection Mirror (9) • Blind Spot Spread (4) • Self-Love Mirror (4) • Umbrella Spread (5) • My Roles Spread (5) • Relationship Mirror (7) • Decision Point (6) • Boundaries Mirror (4) • Self-Acceptance Spread (4) • Comfort Zone Spread (4) • Following Your Story (14 days)

Access

After purchase, you will receive your login credentials. Access is valid for 12 months from the date of purchase.

Important Information

Digital Coaching Cards is a tool designed to support self-reflection and personal development. The cards do not provide diagnoses and are not a substitute for psychological, psychiatric, or medical services.
